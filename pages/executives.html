Ada’s Team Executive Committee


The Ada’s Team executive committee is elected every April. There are some descriptions of the positions below but for the actual responsibilities please see our constitution here. The following are the committee members for the 2020-21 academic year:


President:


Responsible for managing the general executive, set a strategic direction for Ada's, creating meeting agendas, and more.

 Helen Gezahegn

VP Administration:


Responsible for organizing and planning meetings, recording meeting minutes, and maintaining internal communications within the Executive Committee.

 Katherine Patenio

VP Academic:


Responsible for scheduling Ada’s Base office hours and tutoring services, collecting information regarding student struggles, and advocating for changes on behalf of students to the department.

 Amress Almonte

VP Finance:


Responsible for reimbursememts, setting a budget, tracking all incoming and outgoing cash flow, and applying for grants used by the student group for operations.

 Amanda Nguyen

VP Digital Media:


Responsible for maintaining the website, blog, social media, and Ada’s Base whiteboard/bulletin board. They would also collaborate with VP Communications in making posters and advertising for events.

 JuSong Baek

VP Communications:


Responsible for tracking membership, sending out and crafting weekly/monthly emails, collaborating with VP Digital Media in making posters and advertising for events, and replying to emails.

 Celina Sheng

VP Campus Outreach


Responsible for acting as a liaison between Ada's Team and other student groups on campus with similar goals or missions. Including but not limited to: Student Group Services, BearsDen, and WISEST.

 Riona Wiberg

VP Industry Outreach


Responsible for liaising with companies and industry partners to organize events at the University of Alberta and helping with organizing events on campus to enrich members’ university experiences.

 Dorsa Nahid

VP Conference - Operations:


They would be responsible for booking accommodations at conferences in foreign cities, coordinating flights to and from the event, and handling other logistics in the conference city.

 Chelsea Hong

VP Conference - Relations:


Responsible for determining what the Department would like to see from Ada's Team during conferences, bringing any promotional materials to the conference, and hosting events at the conference.

 Monica Bui

VP Conference - Finance:


Responsible for applying for grants, talking to professors regarding fundraising for conferences, and handling reimbursements for students attending conferences.

 Amy Xiang

Mascot:


Responsible for being adorable and lifting our spirits during midterms
